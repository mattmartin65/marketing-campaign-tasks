üìß Email Campaign Best Practices

Timing & Cadence

Campaign Type Email Spacing Total Duration Best Send Days Best Send Times
Welcome/Onboarding 2-3 days apart 1-2 weeks Tue, Wed, Thu 10am or 2pm
Nurture Series 4-7 days apart 3-4 weeks Tue, Wed, Thu 10am local time
Re-engagement 5-7 days apart 2-3 weeks Tuesday 10am
Industry/B2B 7-10 days apart 4-6 weeks Tue, Wed 9-11am
For medical professionals specifically:
  • Avoid Mondays (clinic catch-up) and Fridays (winding down)
  • Mid-morning (10-11am) works well ‚Äî after morning rounds, before lunch
  • Consider time zones across Australia

Subject Line Best Practices

  • Keep under 50 characters (mobile preview)
  • Use numbers when relevant ("Save 30 min/day")
  • Create urgency without being spammy
  • Personalize with name or specialty when possible
  • A/B test subject lines (Brevo supports this)

Examples:

‚úÖ "Dr {{first_name}}, streamline your PBS forms" ‚úÖ "New gastro content this week" ‚úÖ "3 ways to save time on PBS authorities"
‚ùå "URGENT: Don't miss this opportunity!!!" ‚ùå "You won't BELIEVE this offer" ‚ùå "Act now before it's too late"

Email Structure

  1. Clear single CTA ‚Äî one main action per email
  2. Scannable format ‚Äî short paragraphs, bullet points
  3. Mobile-first ‚Äî 60%+ open on mobile
  4. Preheader text ‚Äî use the preview text strategically
  5. Unsubscribe visible ‚Äî legal requirement, builds trust

A/B Testing Strategy

Test one element at a time:

  1. Week 1-2: Subject lines (send to 20% of list, winner to 80%)
  2. Week 3-4: Send times (morning vs afternoon)
  3. Week 5-6: CTA button text/color
  4. Ongoing: Content length, personalization

Automation Rules (Brevo)

Recommended automations:
  • If opened but didn't click ‚Üí Send follow-up after 24h
  • If clicked but didn't convert ‚Üí Alert sales team or send different offer
  • If converted ‚Üí Exit campaign, enter onboarding sequence
  • If no opens after 2 emails ‚Üí Move to re-engagement list
  • After 90 days inactive ‚Üí Remove from active lists

Budget & Bidding

Campaign Type Recommended Daily Budget Bidding Strategy
New campaign (testing) $20-50/day Maximize Clicks
Established campaign $50-100/day Target CPA
High-intent keywords $100+/day Manual CPC
Medical/B2B typically:
  • Higher CPC ($5-20 per click) but higher conversion value
  • Start with Maximize Clicks for 2 weeks to gather data
  • Switch to Target CPA once you have 15+ conversions

Keyword Match Types

Match Type Use For Example
[exact] High-intent, proven converters [pbs authority form]
"phrase" Moderate intent "gastro pbs forms"
broad Discovery (with caution) medical practice software

Negative Keywords (Add Immediately)

Always exclude: free, jobs, salary, training, course, student, sample, template, download, pdf (unless relevant to your offer)

Ad Copy Best Practices

  • Headline 1: Include main keyword
  • Headline 2: Benefit or differentiator
  • Headline 3: CTA or trust signal
  • Descriptions: Expand on benefits, include CTA

Required Ad Extensions:

  • Sitelinks (4-6 links to key pages)
  • Callouts (free trial, Australian-made, etc.)
  • Structured snippets (specialties covered)
  • Call extension (if phone support available)

Landing Page Requirements

  • Message match (headline matches ad)
  • Single clear CTA
  • Mobile optimized
  • Fast load time (<3 seconds)
  • Trust signals (logos, testimonials, security badges)
  • Form above the fold (or clear path to it)

Content Mix (80/20 Rule)

  3. Polls ‚Äî high engagement, good for market research
  4. Text-only with hook ‚Äî personal stories, industry takes

‚úÖ Quick Checklists

Before Sending an Email

  • Subject line under 50 characters
  • Preheader text set
  • Single clear CTA
  • Links have UTM parameters
  • Mobile preview checked
  • Unsubscribe link visible
  • Test email sent to self
  • A/B test configured (if applicable)

Before Launching Google Ads

  • Conversion tracking installed
  • Negative keywords added
  • Ad extensions set up
  • Landing page loads fast (<3s)
  • Landing page message matches ad
  • Daily budget set
  • Geographic targeting correct
  • All 15 headlines filled

Before Posting on LinkedIn

  • Hook in first 2 lines
  • Value provided (not just promotional)
  • CTA included
  • 3-5 relevant hashtags
  • Links have UTM parameters
  • Image/video is high quality
  • Posting at optimal time

Monthly Review

  • Review email metrics, adjust subject lines
  • Review Google Ads search terms, add negatives
  • Review LinkedIn top posts, replicate success
  • Clean email list (remove bounces, inactive)
  • Update ad copy if CTR dropping
  • Check landing page conversion rate
  • Plan next month's content calendar
